- Our Story
Established in 2014 in Los Angeles California by Fred Ramirez a.k.a. Von Knox  with the purpose of showcasing all bearded men with character from your next door neighbor to the football superstar. A community was built in Social Media and Our Brotherhood was born. It has organically grown into a WORLDWIDE BROTHERHOOD through our gatherings, large and small around the world.
We now have 168 Chapters in 38 countries making BEARDED VILLAINS THE LARGEST BEARD CLUB IN MANKIND HISTORY, Our Chapters activities are Local Community support, Charity Work,Beard Competitions, Charity Events, Families and Members Support.
